About bledsoe-county-correctional-complex

1045 Horsehead Rd, Pikeville, TN 37367

If you are looking for a job, this is the place to be. Keep in mind it is not for everyone, but if you can handle it, there are many perks to the job. One mission, one message, one team is how we operate. Our mission is to operate safe and secure facilities and provide effective community supervision in support of successful reentry.

TDOC Tennessee State Careers

Working in corrections is a challenging and demanding profession that requires professionalism, resilience, and strong interpersonal skills. Correctional staff are responsible for maintaining safety and security within facilities while also managing individuals from diverse backgrounds, often in high‑stress situations. The job involves enforcing rules, de‑escalating conflicts, and remaining alert at all times, but it also offers opportunities to make a positive impact by promoting accountability, rehabilitation, and respect. Despite long hours and emotional strain, working in corrections can be rewarding for those who value public service, teamwork, and the responsibility of helping maintain order within the justice system.
