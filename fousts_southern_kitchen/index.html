classic mac & cheese

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Cook macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt 1/4 cup butter over medium heat. Stir in flour and cook for about 1 minute until it forms a roux.
  3. Gradually whisk in milk, ensuring there are no lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens, about 5 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in cheddar cheese and Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir until well combined.
  6. Pour the mac and cheese into a greased baking dish. In a small bowl, melt 2 tablespoons of butter and mix with bread crumbs. Sprinkle the breadcrumb mixture evenly over the mac and cheese.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbly. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
