About Foust Southern Kitchen

Foust Southern Kitchen is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving delicious southern cuisine for over 20 years. Our mission is to provide our customers with authentic southern dishes made from the freshest ingredients. My Story

My name is Matthew Foust and I have always had a passion for cooking. Growing up in the south, I was exposed to a variety of delicious dishes that inspired me to dream of pursue a career in the culinary arts. After years of cooking for my family and friends and honing my skills, I have dreamed of opening my own restaurant to share my love of southern cuisine with others. Foust Southern Kitchen is the culmination of my dreams and hard work, that I hope will bring joy to all who visit when i decide to follow that dream in twenty years once i retire from corrections.

Our menu features a variety of classic southern dishes, including fried chicken, shrimp and grits, collard greens, and cornbread. We also offer a selection of homemade desserts, such as pecan pie and banana pudding. At Foust Southern Kitchen, we strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere where our customers can enjoy delicious food and create lasting memories with their loved ones.
