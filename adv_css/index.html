symptoms

some symptoms some people may not be aware of are a depressed mood. if Maybe your feeling sad or empty or on the verge of tears reach out to someone. Another common symptom is loosing interest or pleasure in something you enjoyed doing. If you are experiencing unexplained weight loss or weight gain, that is a symptom! If you have trouble sleeping or being overly tired, you may have depression. Another symptom is a lack of concentration or difficulty with decision making.
